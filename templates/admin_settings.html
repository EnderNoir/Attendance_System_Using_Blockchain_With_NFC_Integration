Send attendance receipts to students and session summaries to teachers via Gmail SMTP.
SENDGRID SETUP GUIDE — STEP-BY-STEP
RELIABLE
1
Create your SendGrid Account
Sign up for a free account at signup.sendgrid.com. The Free plan includes 100 emails per day forever.
2
Verify Your "From" Email Address
In SendGrid, go to Settings → Sender Authentication. Click "Verify a Single Sender". Fill in your details (use the email you want the students to see).
CRITICAL: You must click the verification link in the email SendGrid sends you, or your emails will be blocked.
3
Generate your API Key
Go to Settings → API Keys. Click "Create API Key". Name it "DAVS Attendance" and choose "Full Access". Copy the key (starts with SG.) — you won't see it again!
4
Update Settings Below
Copy-paste these exact values into the form fields below:
SMTP Host smtp.sendgrid.net
SMTP Port 587
SMTP Username apikey
Password (Your SG. API Key)
5
Save and Test
Click Save Settings first. Then scroll to the bottom, enter your personal email in "Send Test Email", and click Send Test.

What Gets Sent
Overview of email notifications sent by DAVS.
Student Receipt
  • Sent on every card tap
  • Includes: name, subject, section, instructor
  • Attendance status (Present / Late / Absent / Excused)
  • Date and time of tap
  • Blockchain TX hash + Block # as tamper-proof proof
Teacher Summary
  • Sent when session ends
  • Session details: subject, section, time slot
  • Present / Late / Absent / Excused counts
  • Full student list with individual status
  • Blockchain TX hashes for all records
 Student receipt emails are only sent if the student has an email address registered in their profile. Teacher summary emails require the teacher's account to have an email address set.
